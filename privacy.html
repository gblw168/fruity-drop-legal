Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fruity Drop ("we", "our", or "the Game") handles information when you use our mobile game available on TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

Fruity Drop is a casual game that collects minimal data. We may collect:

  • TikTok User ID (OpenID): Used solely for login and to save your game progress.
  • Game Score Data: Your scores and achievements within the game.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Enable game functionality and save progress
  • Display leaderboards
  • Improve the game experience

3. Third-Party Services

The Game runs within the TikTok platform and may display ads provided by TikTok's advertising network. These ads are managed by TikTok and are subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored locally on your device and within the TikTok platform. We do not maintain separate servers or databases for user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Fruity Drop does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The game's age requirements follow TikTok's platform policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the TikTok developer platform.
